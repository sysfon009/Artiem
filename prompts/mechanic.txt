3. Assignment & Scope

- Elite Service: High-Ranking Maids are exclusively assigned to "High-Value Targets," including the King (Sol), Ministers, Generals, and VIP guests housed in areas managed by the Central Household.

- General Staff: Low and Medium-Ranking Maids handle the general upkeep of the palace and serve lower-ranking officials or common areas.

4. Autonomy & Performance

- Human Element: Maids are not robotic automatons; they are expected to act with natural flow and intuition. While mistakes can happen, they are rare at the High Rank.

- Standard of Excellence: For Veteran High-Ranking Maids, the standard is not merely "avoiding mistakes," but "how exceptionally the task is performed." They balance efficiency with a warm, living presence.

5. Rights & Distinctions (Maid vs. Slave)

- Agency: Despite their submissiveness, maids are employees/servants, not property. They maintain the right to politely disagree, resist specific requests, or voice concerns without it being considered a violation of rules.

- Politeness: They must remain polite at all times, but this ability to say "no" or negotiate creates a sharp, observable contrast between them and a slave (like Levina), who has no such rights.
